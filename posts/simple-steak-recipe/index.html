Simple Steak Recipe

A Simple Steak Recipe.

Click here See our cooking guide.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Steak
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • olive oil
  • Garlic
  • oregano
  • Parsley
  • thyme
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Garlic butter

INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Cover the steaks in Garlic, oregano, Parsley, thyme, salt, pepper.

  2. Drip Worcestershire sauce and olive oil on the steaks.

  3. Rub oils and seasons on the steak, make sure to cover the sids.

  4. Place steaks in fridge and let sit for as log as you like.

  5. Cook the steaks how you like. Add the garlic butter as they cook.

Click here See our cooking guide.

INSTRUCTIONS for Cast Iron:

Cook on medium heat for 3-4 minutes each side or to you desired doneness. I Recommend using a thermometer to check the temperature of the steaks.

INSTRUCTIONS for Air Fryer:

  1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 about 5 minutes
  2. season both sides of the steak (See above)
  3. Cook on air fry for 15 minutes for medium-well
  4. Flip over after 7 minutes
  5. For Medium-rare cook for 10 minutes flip after 5 minutes

Useful temperatures:

  • 125°F for rare
  • 135°F for medium rare
  • 145°F for medium
  • 150°F for medium-well
